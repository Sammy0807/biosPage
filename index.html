• Ghemri Lila
    Lila Ghemri is a professor of Computer Science at Texas Southern University (TSU). She graduated with a Bachelor of Engineering in Computer Engineering from the University of Science and Technology in Algiers and with a PhD in Computer Science from the University of Bristol in England. Her research interests are in online social networks privacy and data analytics. Before joining academia, in 2002, Ghemri worked for industry developing AI applications for machine understanding of human language. Her interest in Human Trafficking stems from her work on analyzing online social networks and the various patterns of interaction between agents and how they can indicate baiting of potential victims.
